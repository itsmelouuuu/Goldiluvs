Company

Goldilocks takes pride in being the leading Filipino-owned Bakeshop in the world. For over 56 years, Goldilocks is proud of its strong local heritage that has been trusted to become part of Filipino home traditions and celebrations. Its extensive retail footprint amasses to over 900 stores nationwide and employs almost 4,000 employees, making Goldilocks the largest bakeshop chain in the Philippines.
